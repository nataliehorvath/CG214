YOU CAN BE YOU

I was in class painting the first time I heard Saint Motel's 'You Can Be You.' I wasn't in love with it the first time I heard it but it slowly grew on me and I now think it's one of the sweetest songs off their new album.
The picture to the left is of my friend Joely and me living up to the name of the song and just being ourselves after the concert. My friends and I celebrated Joely's birthday with balloons that fell from the ceiling after the concert. The concert was phenomenal and the venue- Webster Hall -was amazing!
