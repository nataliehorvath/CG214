MY TYPE

The first time I heard Saint Motel was at a State Park in
Connecticut called Kent Falls last summer. I was having a lunch picnic
with a bunch of friends when the infectious beat of "My Type" came
out of my friends Jambox. The trumpets opening the song
are undeniably fun and the song makes you want to get up and move!
The Unofficial music video is my favorite one. A bunch of people theatrically dancing to the coolest song ever written??? Perfect! I can't even count the number of times I've listened to "My Type" that summer but according to Spotify it was my most played song for 2015.
